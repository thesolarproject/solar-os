# Get Music (Reach)

**Get Music** is Solar’s built-in Soulseek client for searching, streaming, saving, and queuing tracks from the network.

## Basics

- Home shortcut **Get Music** (hidden when offline or when Reach login fails on your network).
- Search with the on-screen keyboard; results support play, save, add to queue, and “find more” suggestions.
- Streaming starts after ~20% of the file is buffered; album art uses local ID3 when enough bytes are cached.

## Sharing

While charging on Wi‑Fi (or while the Reach UI is open), Solar can announce `/Music` and `/Podcasts` to Soulseek peers.

## Tips

- Save Reach temp tracks to your library from the player or queue context menu.
- Use **Find like this** on library tracks to open prefilled searches from ID3 tags.
